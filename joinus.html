Opportunities

Join Us

We are continually on the lookout for driven and passionate individuals to enhance our team!

Postdoctoral candidates:

Individuals possessing a Ph.D. or MD degree in relevant fields who are enthusiastic about our research are encouraged to contact Dr. Zhu at qian.zhu@bcm.edu. Be sure to include your CV and a detailed statement of your research interests.

Graduate students:

Graduate students from Quantitative and Computational Bioscience Graduate Program or/and Genetics and Genomics Graduate Program are encouraged to contact Dr. Zhu at qian.zhu@bcm.edu to discuss potential rotation opportunities.

Undergraduate students:

People who are eager to engage in our research may directly contact Dr. Zhu at qian.zhu@bcm.edu to explore available research opportunities.
